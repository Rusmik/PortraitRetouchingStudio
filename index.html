We Offer Monument Retouching and More

Our studio specializes in monument retouching services. While our primary focus is on preserving and enhancing the beauty of monuments, we also offer a range of additional services. These include professional photo retouching, image processing, quality enhancement, and size adjustment, among others. Our dedication to excellence ensures that every project, whether it's monument restoration or general photo editing, receives the utmost attention to detail and care.✨

Retouching for engraving on granite memorials

Included options:
  • Full retouching
  • Hairstyle correction
  • Clothing adjustment or change
  • Pose adjustment (if necessary)
  • Choice of black background, halo effect, or your preferred background
Prices (per person):
  • Chest up: $15
  • Full height: $25
How to order:
  1. Contact: Reach out via email, social networks, or our website form.
  2. Submit Images: Provide the portraits for retouching.
  3. Provide Details: Share all necessary instructions for precise results.
  4. Retouching: We'll enhance and clean up the images, ensuring a natural look.
  5. Review & Approval: You'll receive a soft proof for review. Revisions are accommodated until satisfaction.
  6. Finalization: Once approved, we prepare the final files for engraving or provide a watermarked image for new clients. If you're a new user, you'll receive an unwatermarked image after payment.
  7. Payment: Complete payment for our services.
  8. Feedback: Your feedback is invaluable for our improvement.
